# QuickDrop

**Drop it. Done.**

Fast, secure file transfer between your phone and PC over local WiFi. No cloud, no accounts, no nonsense.

![Windows](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Windows-blue) ![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-Proprietary-red) ![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.0.0-green)

## Features

- **Instant local transfer** — Send files between phone and PC over your WiFi network
- **PIN security** — 4-digit PIN protects every transfer session
- **QR code connect** — Scan from your phone to connect instantly
- **Zero setup** — No accounts, no cloud, no sign-ups
- **Dark & light themes** — Your eyes, your choice
- **Custom save location** — Choose where received files land
- **Works offline** — Everything stays on your local network

## How It Works

1. Open QuickDrop on your PC
2. Scan the QR code with your phone (or enter the URL manually)
3. Enter the 4-digit PIN shown in the app
4. Drop files from either device

Your files transfer directly between devices over your local WiFi — nothing leaves your network.

## FAQ

**Is it free?**
Yes. If you find it useful, you can [buy me a coffee](https://buymeacoffee.com/dreadedgoose).

**Does it work without internet?**
Yes — QuickDrop only uses your local WiFi network. No internet connection required.

**Is it safe?**
Files transfer directly between your devices over your local network. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Each session is protected by a random PIN.

**What file types are supported?**
Any file type — photos, videos, documents, archives, anything.

**What about Mac/Linux?**
Windows only for now. Cross-platform support may come in future versions.
